Our Story

From a Vision to a Global Lab

2019
The Foundations

Ramkumar founded BootLabs with a vision to simplify cloud adoption with the right tools and technology.

2020
Cloud Consulting

Began helping businesses scale with efficient cloud solutions and accelerating digital transformation through modern DevOps practices.

2021
Pioneering Cloud for Early Clients

Delivered a multi-cloud orchestration solution migrating 150+ applications. Established partnerships with Mahindra & Mahindra and Thoughtworks.

2022
Platform Engineering

Launched Sailor — a multi-cloud orchestrator built for enterprise scale. Team grew to 50+ engineers.

2023
Stepping into AI

Ventured into AI platforms and GenAI. Launched Guardian — a unified vulnerability management platform with AI-driven remediation.

2024
Expansion Beyond Borders

Opened new offices in Mumbai and UAE. Engineering center established in Bangalore. Global customer base across US, Europe, and Philippines.

2025
Expanding Horizons

Strengthened OEM partnerships for global AI adoption. Launched AI-enabled Managed Services (AIMS).

2026 & Beyond
A Global AI Engineering Force

200+ member team, serving 30+ clients including major financial services institutions and government entities. Continuing to push the frontier of AI-native enterprise transformation.
