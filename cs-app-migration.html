150+ Applications Migrated in 12 Months — Zero Rollbacks, Enterprise DevSecOps Built from the Ground Up

A large multi-business conglomerate needed to migrate 150+ applications on an accelerated timeline, with virtually no DevSecOps foundation in place. BootLabs delivered the migration and transformed how the entire organisation ships software.

About the Client
Client A Large Multi-Business Conglomerate
Industry Automotive & Industrial
Scale 150+ group companies, multiple technology stacks, thousands of engineers
Challenge Scope Large-scale cloud migration + DevSecOps transformation
The Challenge

150+ applications, an accelerated deadline, and no DevSecOps foundation

The client needed to move fast — but the combination of scale, legacy complexity, and an immature DevSecOps practice made every decision high-risk. The programme had to succeed with no room for rollbacks and no tolerance for downtime.

Challenge 01

Scale & Complexity of Migration

150+ applications spanning legacy monoliths, microservices, and multi-tier systems across different technology stacks and teams — all requiring migration on an accelerated, minimum-risk timeline.

Challenge 02

No DevSecOps Foundation

Limited DevSecOps setup meant applications were not cloud-ready. Manual deployments, no automated security scanning, and no secrets management created risk at every stage of the programme.

Challenge 03

Zero Downtime Requirements

Database migrations required zero downtime. End-to-end observability — monitoring, logging, and alerting — needed to be in place from the moment workloads went live.

Our Approach

A migration factory, built with security and observability at every layer

01

Modernisation Pathfinding

Assessed each application and defined a modernisation path: containerise, re-platform, or refactor. Built a migration factory approach to run parallel workstreams without cross-team dependency — enabling safe, high-velocity delivery across 150+ apps.

02

Enterprise DevSecOps Induction

Inducted DevSecOps across the organisation: automated security scanning in CI/CD, secrets management, templatised rollback strategies, and container security policies standardised across all teams — transforming a high-risk programme into a controlled one.

03

Container-Native Deployment

Deployed applications to Kubernetes with automated containerised pipelines, GitOps workflows, and Helm-managed rollout strategies — ensuring repeatability and auditability at every step. Every deployment was version-controlled and reproducible.

04

Observability from Day One

Deployed full-stack observability covering metrics, logs, and alerts across all migrated workloads — so the operations team had visibility from the moment each application went live. No workload went to production without its observability stack in place.

Results

150+ migrations completed. Zero rollbacks. An organisation transformed.

150+

100%
Organisation covered by DevSecOps practice by programme end — transforming how thousands of engineers develop and ship software

Every application migrated on schedule, with zero rollbacks — an extraordinary outcome for a programme of this scale and complexity. Parallel workstreams, templatised deployments, and enforced observability standards meant that each migration built on the last, compounding quality and speed throughout the programme.

Beyond the migration itself, the DevSecOps practice that was built matured and scaled to cover the entire engineering organisation, fundamentally changing how teams develop and ship software. The migration was the beginning, not the end, of the transformation.
