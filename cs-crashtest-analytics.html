Crash Test Analytics Platform That Cut Compliance Reporting from Weeks to Hours

A leading automotive OEM had no structured way to query or analyse data from physical crash tests. BootLabs built the platform that transformed raw sensor data into a structured analytics layer — with automated compliance reporting.

Industry
Automotive / Manufacturing
Services
Full Stack Development, Data Engineering, Analytics
Location
India
The Challenge

Crash test data locked in spreadsheets and proprietary equipment outputs

A leading automotive OEM had no structured way to query or analyse data from physical crash tests. Safety engineers were working from Excel spreadsheets, manually extracting data from proprietary test equipment outputs. Every comparison across vehicle variants, impact zones, or regulatory standards meant building a new pivot table from scratch. And when it came to NCAP and regional compliance reporting, the process required weeks of manual data extraction, formatting, and verification — a bottleneck that blocked every major vehicle programme launch. BootLabs built the platform that transformed raw crash test sensor data into a structured, queryable analytics layer — with automated compliance reporting mapped to multiple regulatory frameworks.

Client Snapshot
Type Large Automotive Manufacturer
Industry Automotive / R&D
Scale Multiple vehicle platforms, global safety certification requirements
Project Crash test data platform and compliance automation
Business Challenges

What was holding them back

01
Unstructured Sensor Data

Crash test equipment outputs raw, high-volume proprietary data. Without a structured ingestion layer, engineers had no scalable way to query or compare results across tests.

02
Manual Compliance Reporting

Producing NCAP and regional regulatory compliance reports required weeks of manual data extraction, formatting, and verification — a bottleneck before every major vehicle programme launch.

03
No Cross-Test Comparability

Safety engineers couldn't slice results by impact zone, vehicle variant, or regulatory standard without building custom Excel pivot tables for every analysis — making cross-programme comparison nearly impossible.

Our Approach

How we solved it

01
Sensor Data Ingestion Pipeline

Built a structured ingestion layer that normalises raw outputs from multiple crash test equipment vendors into a single queryable data model, preserving full test provenance and traceability.

02
Analytics Data Layer

Designed a structured data layer with dimensional modelling around crash events, vehicle variants, impact zones, and regulatory standards — enabling consistent, repeatable queries across programmes.

03
Interactive Engineering Dashboards

Built a web-based analytics interface giving safety engineers on-demand access to test results — filterable by impact zone, vehicle variant, speed parameter, and regulatory standard. Replaced Excel entirely.

04
Automated Compliance Reporting

Implemented templated report generation mapped to NCAP and regional safety frameworks. Engineers select the vehicle programme and target market; the system generates the formatted compliance report.

The Outcomes

Results that proved the approach

90%

Regulatory Frameworks Automated

Sensor Data Traceability

What once took weeks now takes hours. Safety engineers across multiple vehicle programmes can generate fully formatted NCAP and regional compliance reports on demand — traceable from raw sensor output to final submission. The platform covers 10+ regulatory frameworks including NCAP, BIS, and regional standards, and every data point carries a complete audit trail back to its source equipment output.

Business Impact

What changed for the organisation

Faster Programme Launches

Safety certification no longer blocks vehicle programme timelines. What once took weeks to produce now delivers results in hours, keeping launches on schedule.

Engineering Focus

Safety engineers spend time analysing data and identifying improvement areas rather than wrangling spreadsheets and formatting reports.

Cross-Programme Intelligence

The structured data layer enables comparison across vehicle platforms and test series for the first time — surfacing patterns not visible in isolated reports.

Audit-Ready Output

Every compliance report is traceable to its source sensor data with a full audit trail — reducing risk during regulatory submissions.
