Capabilities

What we offer.

Four security engineering capabilities that keep your code, cloud, and data safe — without slowing your teams down.

01
DevSecOps Integration

Security scanning embedded directly in your CI/CD pipelines — SAST, DAST, container scanning, and secrets management — shifting security left so vulnerabilities are caught at the commit, not in production.

02
Vulnerability Management

Continuous CVE scanning across your codebase, containers, and dependencies — with automated triage, risk scoring, patch prioritisation, and SLA-backed remediation workflows for your engineering teams.

03
Cloud Security Posture (CSPM)

Automated misconfiguration detection, CIS and NIST benchmarking, and policy-as-code enforcement across your AWS, Azure, and GCP environments — with real-time dashboards and auto-remediation playbooks.

04
Security Automation & SOAR

SOAR playbooks, threat intelligence integration, and automated incident response — reducing mean time to respond (MTTR) for security events and freeing your security team from manual, repetitive triage work.
